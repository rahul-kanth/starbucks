Partners for lifelong learners

What started as a conversation between former Starbucks CEO Howard Schultz and
Arizona State University President Michael M. Crow led to a shared philosophy and the
idea of providing access to lifelong learning worldwide. And they decided to do just
that, starting with Starbucks employees (“partners”). Welcome to Starbucks Global
Academy—Powered by ASU.
